### Netflix Data Analysis (EDA Project)

Exploratory Data Analysis on Netflix Movies and TV Shows dataset to understand content trends and patterns.

Key Analysis:
- Movies vs TV Shows distribution
- Content ratings analysis
- Content growth over time
- Top countries producing content
- Top genres analysis
- Pivot table (Ratings vs Type)

Key Insights:
- Netflix has more Movies than TV Shows
- TV-MA is the most common rating
- Content growth increased after 2016
- USA dominates content production
- Drama and International Movies are most common
